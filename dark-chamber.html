Game Concept

Dark Chamber is a 3D multiplayer co-op horror puzzle game with a first person view for Windows PC with a maximum of 2 players per game.

In this game, the players' objective is to solve a chain of puzzles, where they get progressively harder, at which point an enemy will start to chase the players. By completing the puzzles and successfully avoiding the enemy, the players will be able to escape the map and end the game.
