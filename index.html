About me

My name is Tomás Morais, I am 21 years old and I am a Gameplay Programmer from Portugal!

I am currently on Erasmus at Coventry University studying Games Technologies and on the last year of the Games Development BSc at IADE - Creative University.

My passion for games has been huge since little and have carried on with this for all of these years which have led me to where I am now.

Now I can deliver the same passion and feelings the games I've played have brought me to others.

As an avid gamer, my hardwork will always be to perfect my work and skill and my commitment will be always with the player in order to make them have fun and feel connected with my games.
