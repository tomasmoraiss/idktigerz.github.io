Game Concept

Wild Angles is a single player survival adventure game for Windows PC and MacOS.

In this game you take on the role of Michelle, a wilderness photographer, who was sent to a hidden jungle in Singapore to take photos of the animals and plants living there.

You have seven days to complete your diary and are accompanied by your baby tiger companion.

Use the nightvision filter on your camera to get around during the night but be careful to not use all of your battery!

Some animals and plants become active during certain times. Use your camp to skip ahead of time and refiil your battery recharges. Can you survive?

Project done for a university project.
