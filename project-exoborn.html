Game Concept

Project: Exoborn is a single player, 2D action rpg, space exploration game with top down perspective compatible with Windows PC, where the player combats, explores, and searches for resources in order to upgrade his suit to become stronger.

You are a human infused with genetic modifications that is searching and killing enemies in order to protect the core of the solar system.

As the enemies become more and more impatient to steal the core of the solar system , they start sending more and more spaceships to enlarge their numbers in hopes of destroying the core.

The player must find the enemy's spaceships and kill the core of each spaceship in order to stop the their attack.

Project done for a university project.
